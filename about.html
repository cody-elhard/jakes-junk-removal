About Jake's Junk Removal

Your trusted partner in clearing the clutter

Serving Fargo-Moorhead with Pride

Jake's Junk Removal has quickly become one of the most trusted names in the Fargo-Moorhead area for professional junk removal and hauling services.

What started as a simple idea to help neighbors clear out their unwanted items has grown into a full-service junk removal operation serving residential and commercial customers throughout the FM metro area. Our commitment to customer satisfaction, fair pricing, and environmental responsibility sets us apart from the competition.

Every job we take on is handled with the same level of care and professionalism, whether we're removing a single piece of furniture or clearing out an entire estate. We understand that letting go of items can be emotional, and we treat every customer's belongings with respect.

Our team takes pride in being part of this community. We live here, we work here, and we're committed to keeping Fargo-Moorhead clean. Whenever possible, we donate usable items to local charities and recycle materials to minimize our environmental impact.
