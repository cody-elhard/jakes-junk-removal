From Tragedy to Triumph

Jake Bacon was an elite high school hockey goalie with dreams of a professional career when tragedy struck‚Äîhis father, Chris, died in a car accident when Jake was just 16.

The loss shattered his world, leading him down a difficult path of drug abuse that would consume several years of his life. Through it all, his mother Michelle remained his rock, supporting him even in his darkest moments.

Finding Love, Faith, and Sobriety

Everything began to change in 2022 when Jake moved to Fargo and met Dessirae Thompson at his apartment complex. With her unwavering support and encouragement, Jake committed to sobriety, learning that setbacks were part of the process. He also discovered faith and began working at Same Day Mattress, where his boss Kyle Jaeger encouraged him to nurture his spiritual life. Today, Jake has two years of sobriety and is engaged to marry Dessirae.

Building a Business from the Ground Up

Channeling the same resilience and work ethic that made him an elite athlete, Jake launched Jake's Junk Removal Service in May 2023 with just a 2002 GMC Sierra pickup. What started as a side hustle quickly grew into a thriving full-time business.
